Linux Installation

Install via Flatpak (Stable):

flatpak remote-add --if-not-exists futr-stable https://flatpak.futrnostr.com/futr-stable.flatpakrepo
 
flatpak install com.futrnostr.futr

Or for continuous builds:

flatpak remote-add --if-not-exists futr-continuous https://flatpak.futrnostr.com/futr-continuous.flatpakrepo
 
flatpak install com.futrnostr.futr

Free and Open Source

Download and use our Nostr client for free. As an open-source project, you can explore and contribute to the code, ensuring transparency and community-driven improvements.

What is Nostr?

Nostr is a decentralized protocol for private, censorship-resistant communication. It allows direct connections between users, ensuring your conversations are free from third-party interference.

Secure Group Chats

Create and join secure public and private groups with NIP-29 support. Enjoy end-to-end encryption for all your group conversations, keeping your chats confidential.

Encrypted Messaging

All messages are encrypted to ensure privacy. Communicate securely, knowing that only your intended recipients can access your conversations.

Voice and Video Calls

Make high-quality voice and video calls directly from the app. Stay connected with seamless multimedia interactions for both personal and professional use.

Cross-Platform Native App

Built with Haskell and Qt5, our client offers a native experience on Linux, Windows, macOS, Android, and iOS, providing optimal performance across all devices.
